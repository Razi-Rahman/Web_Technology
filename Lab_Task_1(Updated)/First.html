Pheonix Travel Agency

Prepared by MD. SYDUR RAHMAN

Pheonix Travel Agency Management System 1.0 is a private retailer or public service that provides travel and tourism-related services to the general public on behalf of accommodation or travel suppliers to offer different kinds of travelling packages for each destination.


Pheonix Travel Agency Management System 1.0 can provide outdoor recreation activities, airlines, car rentals, cruise lines, hotels, railways, travel insurance, package tours, insurance, guide books, VIP airport lounge access, arranging logistics for luggage and medical items delivery for travelers upon request, public transport timetables, car rentals services. Pheonix Travel Agency Management System 1.0 can also serve as general sales agents for airlines that do not have offices in a specific region.
